Remembering

In memory of all our friends

To the classmates who walked these halls beside us and are no longer here — you are not forgotten. Sixty years on, we still carry your friendship, your laughter, and your place in the Class of 1966. As we gather again, we hold you close, and we leave a seat at the table for every friend we've lost along the way.
