Where to stay

Hotels & RV parks

A few nearby places to rest your head. Mention the reunion when you book — and call ahead to confirm rates and availability.

Hotels

The newest hotels sit together just off Airport Park Blvd, about 5–7 minutes south of town.

  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites UkiahCommittee pick

    1270 Airport Park Blvd · (707) 462-1514 · 4.6★

    Newest and highest-rated of the group. Free breakfast, pet-friendly, and EV charging.
  • Hampton Inn Ukiah

    1160 Airport Park Blvd · (707) 462-6555 · 4.3★

    Spacious rooms, pool and hot tub, reliable.
  • Fairfield by Marriott Ukiah

    1140 Airport Park Blvd · (707) 463-3600 · 4.0★

    Clean, recently renovated, standard Marriott.

RV parks

  • Fairgrounds RV ParkCommittee pick

    Redwood Empire Fairgrounds · 1055 N State St · (707) 463-2667 · 4.2★

    Best-rated and most central. Full hookups (50-amp, water, sewer), pull-throughs, and easy Highway 101 access.
  • Ukiah Mobile Home & RV Park

    4951 N State St · (707) 380-8798 · 3.8★

    North of town. Office hours are limited (Thursday and Friday only), so please call ahead.
  • Blue Lakes Village

    5178 CA-20, Upper Lake · (707) 348-4895 · 4.2★

    A nicer park, but about 25 minutes east toward Lake County — a scenic drive rather than a quick one.

Remembering

In memory of all our friends

To the classmates who walked these halls beside us and are no longer here — you are not forgotten. Sixty years on, we still carry your friendship, your laughter, and your place in the Class of 1966. As we gather again, we hold you close, and we leave a seat at the table for every friend we've lost along the way.
